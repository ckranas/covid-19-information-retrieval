Bibliography of the current world literature



This bibliography is compiled by immunologists from the journals listed at the end of this publication. It is based on literature entered into our database between 1st April 1991 and 31st March 1992 (articles are generally added to the database about two and a half months after publication). In addition, the bibliography contains every paper annotated by reviewers; these references were obtained from a variety of bibliographic databases and published between the beginning of the review period and the time of going to press.
